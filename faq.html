Frequent questions

Simple answers to your most common questions

Which events do I get a discount to?

All Party with a Local events in Amsterdam - 2 Boat Parties, 2 Thirsty Thursdays & 1 Rooftop Sessions with live music each month.

See our events calendar for dates and our Facebook events for details. Or see some photos from our recent events - always a lot of fun!

How much discount do I get?

20% on Boat Parties & Rooftop Sessions, so:

  • Boat Parties = €20 (instead of €25)
  • Rooftop Sessions = €8 (instead of €10) Includes 2 craft beers as well as the live music

Plus:

  • Thirsty Thursdays = Free (instead of €5) Includes a welcome cocktail worth €11

How much value do I get?

If you came to 2 Thirsty Thursdays, 2 Boat Parties & a Rooftop Session in a month you would get €34 worth of value (plus exclusive 1st access to really great events).

Even if you only came to one Thirsty Thursday in that month you’d still get €11 of value, which is less than the monthly subscription - good deal!

How will I get 1st access to these events?

We’ll send you an email before the event is announced, with a discount, 3 days before we announce it publicly - which gives you a chance to get 1st tickets.

How can I renew?

We’ll be setting up auto-renew in the future, but for now you can either buy a 1 month or a 3 month subscription. We’ll remind you before it ends to renew (or not).

How can I cancel?

You can cancel at anytime - as we don’t have auto-renew yet, you can simply let the subscription expire.

Do I need this subscription to use the Party with a Local app?

No, that remains free. Subscriptions for events are separate.

How can I cancel?

You can cancel at anytime - as we don’t have auto-renew yet, you can simply let the subscription expire.

I don’t live in Amsterdam, can I get a subscription in my city?

Not yet. Amsterdam is first and other cities in Europe, Brazil & the US will follow. If you think we should add your city, send us an email: info@partywithalocal.com
